About Me

I am a 3rd year PhD student at the Department of Economics, University of Oklahoma. My interest lies in the macroeconomic issues related to housing price and aging.

My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between demographic changes and housing markets, with particular emphasis on how aging populations impact housing prices and macroeconomic stability.
